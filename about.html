Children Deserve to be Children

When a child loses a parent, their whole world is turned upside down. First comes the emotional trauma of losing the one who truly loved them, protected them and cared for them; knowing nothing can ever truly fill that void. Then, the other hardships appear: how to find enough food, pay for rent, school, and health care. The question becomes: without the basics to survive, how will they ever build a better future? This is where we all come in. By making donations and raising awareness to their concerns, we play a large role in showing them they are not on their own. With the donations, they can spend less time worrying about their next meal, and more time building a future for themselves.

Our Story

Alif Donations began with a group of friends from Ottawa, Ontario that learned of Islamic Relief's "Orphan Sponsorship Program". What started as a small crowdfunding initiative among a group of friends, quickly gained attention among the local Ottawa community.
